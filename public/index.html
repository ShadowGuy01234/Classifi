What is Structify AI?

Structify is an intelligent document classification platform designed to automate the organization of unstructured datasets. It combines robust AI models with user feedback to deliver exceptional accuracy and adaptability.

Easy Document Classification

How Structify Works?

Step 1: Upload Your Documents

Start by uploading a ZIP file containing your documents.

Step 2: Automated Classification

The system analyzes the content and categorizes each document.

Step 3: User Review & Feedback

Review results, provide feedback, and retrain for better accuracy.

Step 4: Download Classified Documents

Get organized files in category-specific folders.
